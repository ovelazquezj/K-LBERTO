## Acknowledgment of Previous Work

K-LBERTO is an adaptation based on the following works:

### K-BERT (Original Work)

Paper: K-BERT: Enabling Language Representation with Knowledge Graph
Authors: Wang, B., Xie, Z., Ruan, Z., Wang, S., & Wang, K. (2019)
Repository: https://github.com/thunlp/K-BERT
License: MIT License
Copyright (c) 2019 THUNLP

### BETO (Spanish BERT Model)

Paper: Spanish Pre-trained BERT Model and Evaluation Data
Authors: Canete, J., Chaperon, G., Fuentes, R., Ho, J. H., Kang, H., & Pérez, J. (2020)
Repository: https://github.com/dccuchile/beto
License: Apache 2.0 License
Copyright (c) 2020 Department of Computer Science, University of Chile

### TASS Dataset (Spanish Sentiment Analysis)

Conference: TASS 2019 (Taller de Análisis de Sentimientos en Redes Sociales)
Citation: Villena-Román, C., García-Morera, J., Lana-Serrano, S., González-Cristóbal, J. C., & Martínez-Cámara, E. (2019)
Repository: https://www.aclweb.org/anthology/W19-5305/
License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International
